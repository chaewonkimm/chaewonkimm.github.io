Hi, I’m an undergraduate at Kookmin University, majoring in AI, Big Data & Management with a minor in Computer Science. This past summer, I was an AI research intern at the University of California, Irvine, working on video action recognition and vision-language models. At KMU, I’ve worked on several computer vision projects to improve visual quality.

My work focuses on computer vision and generative modeling to make visual understanding more reliable. Specifically, I develop methods to detect and correct instabilities and distortions in images and videos, generating consistent, high-quality visual results.

More recently, I’ve been extending this foundation to embodied AI—exploring how high-quality visual perception can better guide and control real-world machine actions.
