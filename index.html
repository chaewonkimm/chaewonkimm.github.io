Hi! I’m an undergraduate at Kookmin University (Seoul, South Korea), majoring in AI, Big Data & Management with a minor in Computer Science. This past summer, I was a research intern at UC Irvine, working on video action recognition and vision–language models. At KMU, I’ve been developing methods that make visual perception more stable and trustworthy in the wild.

So far, my work has focused on:

  • Human-aligned visual perception in images and videos
  • Robust perceptual quality enhancement under imperfect visual inputs
  • Multimodal sensing (e.g., physiological signals)
Recently, I’ve been excited to extend reliable perception to embodied settings, where it can better support decision-making and actions. Looking ahead, I want to go beyond vision by incorporating additional modalities (e.g., touch), with a focus on reliable, human-aligned multisensory perception for real-world and embodied AI.
