MatteViT: High-Frequency-Aware Document Shadow Removal with Shadow Matte Guidance

Chaewon Kim*, Seoyeon Lee, Jonghyuk Park
[Under review] (* Equal contribution)

Paper Code

Overview

Document shadow removal methods often fail to remove shadows cleanly while preserving delicate fine structures such as thin strokes and edges. To address this, we propose MatteViT, which leverages both spatial and frequency-domain information to eliminate shadows while maintaining fine-grained structural details. Specifically, MatteViT introduces a continuous luminance-based shadow matte for precise spatial guidance and a lightweight High-Frequency Amplification Module (HFAM) to enhance high-frequency details during restoration. As a result, MatteViT achieves state-of-the-art performance on the RDD and Kligler benchmarks.
